General

Launch at login
Open PuffLabs automatically when you sign in to your Mac.
Keep running in the background
Closing the window keeps PuffLabs in the menu bar, so it reopens instantly and notifications keep arriving.
Global show/hide shortcut
Press ⌘⇧P from any app to bring PuffLabs to the front and open the command palette. Turn off if it clashes with another app’s shortcut.

Notifications

Show notifications
The master switch for macOS notifications. Choose which types you want below.
Notify me about
Direct messages
Someone sends you a DM or a message in a group DM.
Mentions & keywords
You are @-mentioned, or a message matches one of your keyword alerts.
@channel and @here
A channel-wide announcement to everyone, or everyone currently online.
Other activity
Reminders, scheduled messages and other system alerts.
Test notifications
Send yourself a sample notification. The first one also makes PuffLabs appear in System Settings > Notifications.
Privacy & sound
Show message preview
Show the message text in notifications. Turn off to just say “New message” — handy when screen-sharing.
Silent notifications
Show notifications without playing a sound.
Quiet hours, per-channel muting and sounds live inside the app, set from a channel menu and the bell.

Menu bar & Dock

Show timer in the menu bar
Display today's tracked total next to the menu-bar icon.
Dock unread badge
Show the unread count on the Dock icon.
Bounce the Dock on new activity
Bounce the Dock icon when your unread count goes up.
